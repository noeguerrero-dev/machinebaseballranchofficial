What is the 365 Velo Camp?

This camp is a year round program (Mon-Thurs, times may vary) at the MBR. This program focuses on Arm Care, Strength Training, Conditioning, Velocity Training, and Pitching Mechanics.


This is what MBR is most known for. Everyone who is throwing gas in the RGV most likely either attends or attended this camp. This program was created by trial and error, mistakes and successes, from going to many pro/college camps, pitching camps (Like TBR, Top Velocity, Driveline, Tread Athletics) and other research from my son Noe Guerrero III. This has proven to work time and time again with not only my son going from 81 mph to 95, but with many other MBR clients as well.
