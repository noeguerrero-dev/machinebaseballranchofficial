Rentals

At the MBR we offer batting cages, baseball pitching machine, batting sock stations, pitcher's throwing stations, weight room, and pitcher's bullpen. We also have tractor tires, sledgehammers, ropes, pull-up bars, Australian pull-up bar, and dip bars. An 8 x 8 ft. wall to do med ball exercises. Our facilities also include full nighttime lights.

Hitting Sock Station

These are our hitting sock stations. Players can hit tee or soft toss into these sock stations. Our players at the 365 Velocity Training use these sock stations for ball drills as well.



Pitcher Throwing Station

This is the throwing station. Our pitchers at 365 Velocity Training, use these mats to throw driveline balls and do other sorts of pitching ball drills.



Hitting Tire Station

This is the hitters tire station. Hitters can practice their swing by hitting the tire strengthening their forearms, wrist and arms.



Pitchers Bullpen

Our bullpen at the MBR is perfect for pitchers trying to get some reps in or working on pitches and mechanics. It is a full 60ft 6in away from the plate. Our team at the MBR cleans, and repacks the bullpen with new MLB grade red dirt after every session.



Batting Cage #1

This is our batting cage 1. It is a full length batting cage capable of live throwing/hitting.



Batting Cage #2

This is our second batting cage. This cage can also be split into 2 with a net cutting it in half inside. This allows teams to have 3 separate stations inside the 2 cages.



Lights for Evening/Night Practice

All of MBR can be lit up at night. This is perfect for late evening/night training or practice.
