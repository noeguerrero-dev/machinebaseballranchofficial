Diana Guerrero

Brownsville, Texas
Hanna ECHS
Southeastern Oklahoma State College


Diana Guerrero is a Brownsville native. She played softball most of her life and played varsity at her highscool as well. After graduating she received an offer to play at Laredo College, until Covid hit and they had to shut down the softball program. She decided to attend Southeaster Oklahoma Community College to play softball. Diana has played for the Mountaineers for 2 years and is set to graduate Spring of 2022.
